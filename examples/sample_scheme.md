# Sample Marking Scheme — Mathematics Quiz

Total: 20 marks (5 marks per question)

---

## Q1. Trigonometry (5 marks)

Function: y = -2 cos x

- (a) Amplitude = 2, Period = 2π — **2 marks** (1 each)
- (b) Sketch graph for 0 ≤ x ≤ 2π with key points (0,-2), (π/2,0), (π,2), (3π/2,0), (2π,-2) — **2 marks**
- (c) Label any two correct max/min points — **1 mark**

---

## Q2. Vectors (5 marks)

Parallelogram ABCD, AB = a, AD = b

- (a) AC = a + b — **3 marks** (1 for method, 2 for answer)
- (b) DC = a — **2 marks** (1 for property, 1 for answer)

---

## Q3. Coordinate Geometry (5 marks)

Points A(2,1) and B(6,5)

- (a) Gradient m = 1 — **2 marks** (1 for formula, 1 for answer)
- (b) Equation y = x - 1 — **3 marks** (1 for form, 1 for c, 1 for final)

---

## Q4. Differentiation (5 marks)

f(x) = x³ - 3x² + 2

- (a) dy/dx = 3x² - 6x — **2 marks**
- (b) Critical points (0,2) and (2,-2) — **3 marks** (1 for setting=0, 1 for x values, 1 for coordinates)
